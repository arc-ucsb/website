Lubin Lab

UCSB Deep Space Cosmology Unit

Professor Philip Lubin

Aerospace Research Co-op owes a lot of its growth to Professor Lubin and his team of researchers. Dr. Lubin leads the direction of many different astrophysics and STEM research projects and is contributing to strides made in the fields of physics and engineering.

UCSB Experimental Cosmology Group

Directed by Dr. Philip Lubin

UCSB Experimental Cosmology Group is an experimental astrophysics group primarily focused on studies of the early universe and astrophysical applications of directed energy. UCSB ECG is currently focusing on photon acceleration, laser driven planetary defense systems, and experimentation with resilient microorganisms both in microgravity and outside the solar system.
